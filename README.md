# Season_EPA_Tracker
Welcome! This is my attempt to visualize a season's trends by team from a high level. Below I will further explain some of the parts of the graph.

EPA: This is an oppornent and win probability adjusted expected points added per play, either offensive or defensive. Below is a link to further explain EPA.

https://www.nfeloapp.com/analysis/expected-points-added-epa-nfl

Early Down Passing Rate: This is the rate of first and second down plays that are pass attempts. The league mean is also shown for the respective season.

Win/Loss: The opponent is listed on the bottom of the graph, and the color is if the team in question won or lost the game represented by green and red, respectively.

Bye Week: The Red vertical dashed line represents when the team had a bye week (both in season and post season). This is to determine if there were significant changes in results post bye.

Alternate QB: At the bottom of the graph, there may appear a Red "Alt QB" which represents that the QB who had the most passing attempts for the season did not have the most passing attempts in that particular game. This does not necessarily represent the team's actual "Starting QB", but who played more for them that season.
